Tealogy is an Indian café chain which focuses on tea-based beverages. Tealogy founded in 2018 at Indore, India. Now we have 25 tea café outlets across India. We offer a variety of teas and beverages with some snacks as well. Tealogy's provide the best taste of the kulhad tea & coffee at affordable prices. We always try to provide the best experience to our customers and give complete satisfaction via our food, drinks, and services. We provide in our cafe the best place for sitting where you can enjoy with family and friends make some unforgettable memories. At our café, we are not only serving food and drinks or doing our job. We try to make people happy through serving food which tastes rejuvenate their mood.

We believe that TEA is not a drink, it’s an emotion. For some people, happiness begins from the cup of tea and for others tea restore their normality, or for the rest of people cup of tea lead them to a world of quiet contemplation of life. So, we understand everyone’s definition of tea. We also offer some snacks as well. For the type of snacks, you can check out our Menu .
